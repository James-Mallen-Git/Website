The consensus

The pasta created within the walls of The Boiling Pot is exquisite. The flavors that are created will dance and frolic amongst your tastebuds. The Boiling Pot is about as old-school as you can get in an Italian restaurant – low-lit with plenty of space for socializing. The restaurant is host to massive, massive portions of pasta in generous plates in just about any way you like it. Definitely, with a following among the older crowd in northwest Columbus, expect this place to be full – and loud – as groups enjoy the ambiance and the company. There’s something enduring about finding a classic kind of Italian restaurant that’s all about the substance over form. If you are looking for REAL Italian food look no further than the pot. With every drop of wine to every crumb of food, we bring the taste of Italy to your doorstep.
